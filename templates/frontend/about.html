Our Temple

Shri Laxmi Narsinha Mandir is a sacred place of worship dedicated to Lord Narsinha, the fourth incarnation of Lord Vishnu, and Goddess Laxmi. The temple is located in the serene town of Ranjani, Maharashtra, surrounded by natural beauty and spiritual energy.

The temple architecture reflects traditional Hindu temple design with intricate carvings and sculptures depicting various deities and mythological scenes.
